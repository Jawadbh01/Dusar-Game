How to Play DUSAR

🎮 Game Setup

• 4 players in 2 teams (diagonal partners)

• 13 cards dealt to each player

• One player calls Trump (Rang/Color)

💰 Bidding Phase

• Bid how many tricks you'll win (9-13)

• Or bid "52 Salam" (all tricks)

• Highest bidder calls trump suit

🎴 Card Strength

• Ace is highest, 2 is lowest

• Trump suit beats all other cards

• 2♠ of Trump = Super card (beats Ace)

🏆 Scoring

• If bid 9 & get 9 = You score 9 pts

• If opponent gets more = They score 18 pts

• First to 100+ points wins

📋 Trick Rules

• Must follow suit if you have it

• Highest card of suit led wins

• Trump card beats all other suits
